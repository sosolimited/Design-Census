    The Design Census Data Explorer is a tool that shows patterns and trends in the 2016 AIGA Design Census. With nearly 10,000 responses from across the nation, there are many stories and surprises to be revealed within the data. Find a compelling combination? Download your discoveries to a CSV file and create a visualization of your own.

    This tool was created by Sosolimited, an art, design, and technology studio and the official data visualization partner of the 2016 Design Census.
